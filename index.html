About Us

South Africa's Trusted Radiation Calibration Partner

BruinOu Engineering Consultants is a fully SANAS accredited ionizing radiation calibration laboratory serving hospitals, nuclear facilities, industrial operators, government bodies, and research institutions across South Africa.

Our team of certified medical physicists and radiation protection specialists combines deep technical expertise with a genuine commitment to client service — making complex calibration work simple and stress-free for you.

  • Metrological traceability to NMISA and international NMI standards
  • Fast turnaround times — express services available on request
  • Detailed calibration certificates with full uncertainty budgets
  • On-site calibration services available across South Africa
  • Support with regulatory compliance and radiation protection programs
  • Warm, dedicated client service from first contact to certificate
What We Do

Our Calibration Services

Comprehensive calibration across a wide range of ionizing radiation sources, quantities, and detector types.

Diagnostic & Therapeutic Radiology

Calibration of ionization chambers, electrometers, and dosimeters for diagnostic imaging, CT, fluoroscopy, mammography, and radiation therapy.

Gamma & Beta Dosimetry

Standards & Compliance

Our Accreditation

We operate under the most rigorous quality frameworks so every certificate we issue carries full regulatory confidence.

🏅 SANAS Accreditation

Fully accredited by the South African National Accreditation System (SANAS) under accreditation number CAL 112-12-00, covering a broad scope of ionizing radiation quantities and energy ranges.

📋 ISO/IEC 17025:2017

Our quality management system fully complies with ISO/IEC 17025:2017 — the international standard for testing and calibration laboratory competence and impartiality.

🔗 Metrological Traceability

All calibrations trace back to NMISA (National Metrology Institute of South Africa) and international primary standards through a fully documented, unbroken chain of comparisons.

🔄 Proficiency Testing

We participate regularly in SANAS-approved proficiency testing schemes and international intercomparisons to demonstrate and maintain our ongoing technical competence.
